Forgotten Beasts

===================================================

Forgotten Beasts are randomly generated creatures from the game
Dwarf Fortress. The beasts can range from a metal skinned Tortoise to a
Bird made of steam that spits acid webs.

Here you will be able to chronicle beasts encountered within the game
or generate a custom one. Feel free to browse the news section for game updates
or browse the Open5E Monster API.
